Wildlife Spotlight

Whimbrel Bird

Numenius phaeopus

A migratory bird that makes an extraordinary journey from Siberia to Wondama Bay, West Papua. Each year it travels thousands of kilometers seeking warm tropical habitats in Indonesia.

Distinguished by its long, downward-curving bill, measuring 37–45 cm. In Wondama Bay, they are found on beaches, estuaries, and mudflats at low tide, foraging for small crabs and invertebrates.
